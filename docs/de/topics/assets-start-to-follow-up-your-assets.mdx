Most important starting pages to see how your assets are behaving.

To start optimizing your supply chain it is important to learn the basic navigation on the platform. The platform has a
<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://cloud.sensolus.com/documentation/images/icons/icon-home.png" />
</Frame>
Home view and an
<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://cloud.sensolus.com/documentation/images/icons/icon-admin-section.png" />
</Frame>
Admin view. The Home view provides all the insights, the Admin view is where you configure dashboards, notifications, geozones and much more. You switch between the two views by selecting the icon in the lower left corner on the platform.\
<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://cloud.sensolus.com/documentation/images/userinterface/general_navigation/navigation_home_admin.png" />
</Frame>

Once you have linked the trackers to the assets you want to monitor, you can start exploiting the insights generated by the platform. The four most important pages to start monitoring your assets are the following:

* Asset list
* Asset map
* Asset Status
* Assets trackers list
